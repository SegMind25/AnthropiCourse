---

## 🧠 What is AI Fluency?

AI Fluency is about developing **practical skills, knowledge, insights, and values** that help you interact with AI systems in ways that are:

| Principle | What It Means |
|---|---|
| ⚡ **Effective** | Getting real, useful results from AI interactions |
| 🚀 **Efficient** | Making the most of AI without wasting time or effort |
| ⚖️ **Ethical** | Using AI in ways that are fair, honest, and responsible |
| 🔒 **Safe** | Protecting yourself, others, and your data when using AI |

> *"Being fluent with AI isn't about understanding how AI works under the hood — it's about knowing how to work with it as a true collaborator."*

---

## 🤝 Three Ways We Collaborate with AI

This lesson introduces three distinct modes of human-AI collaboration:

---

### ⚙️ 1. Automation
> *The AI completes specific tasks based on your instructions.*

- You give clear, defined instructions
- AI executes the task independently
- Best for: repetitive, well-defined tasks with predictable outputs
- Example: *"Generate a summary of this document"* or *"Format this data as a CSV"*

---

### 🤝 2. Augmentation
> *You and AI collaborate as creative thinking and task execution partners.*

- You and AI work together in a back-and-forth loop
- AI enhances your thinking rather than replacing it
- Best for: complex problems, creative work, research, and decision-making
- Example: *Brainstorming together, iterating on drafts, exploring different angles of a question*

---

### 🧭 3. Agency
> *You configure AI to work independently on your behalf, establishing its knowledge and behavior patterns rather than just giving it specific tasks.*

- You define the AI's context, goals, and boundaries upfront
- AI operates with greater independence within those parameters
- Best for: ongoing workflows, automated pipelines, and delegated projects
- Example: *Setting up a Claude Project with custom instructions, building an AI agent for a recurring task*

---

## 🔁 How the Three Modes Connect

```
AUTOMATION   →  AI does a task you specify
AUGMENTATION →  You and AI think and work together
AGENCY       →  AI operates on your behalf within boundaries you set
```

As your AI Fluency grows, you'll move fluidly between all three — choosing the right mode for each situation rather than defaulting to just one.

---

## 📝 My Key Takeaways

- [x] AI Fluency = practical skills + knowledge + values for working with AI
- [x] The goal is to be effective, efficient, ethical, and safe — not just fast
- [x] Automation, Augmentation, and Agency are three different collaboration modes
- [x] Fluency means choosing the *right* mode for the right task
- [x] This is a skill that grows — and stays relevant as AI keeps evolving
