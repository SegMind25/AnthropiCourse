## 👋 About This Module

This module covers **AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations** — Anthropic's course built around the research of **Prof. Rick Dakan** (Ringling College of Art & Design) and **Prof. Joseph Feller** (University College Cork).

AI Fluency goes beyond prompt tips and tricks. It's a **lasting framework** for collaborating with AI effectively, efficiently, ethically, and safely — skills that stay relevant as AI keeps evolving.

> *"AI Fluency is not about understanding AI as technology — it's about developing a durable framework for working with AI as a true collaborator."*

## 🏆 The 4D Framework for AI Fluency

The core of everything in this course — developed by **Prof. Rick Dakan** & **Prof. Joseph Feller**:

| Competency | Core Question | What It Covers |
|---|---|---|
| 🎯 **Delegation** | *Should I give this to AI?* | Deciding what humans do vs. what AI does |
| 📝 **Description** | *Am I explaining this clearly?* | Communicating effectively with AI |
| 🔍 **Discernment** | *Is this output good enough?* | Critically evaluating AI responses |
| 🛡️ **Diligence** | *Am I being responsible?* | Using AI ethically and safely |

---

## 🤝 Three Ways to Engage with AI

| Mode | Description |
|---|---|
| ⚙️ **Automation** | The AI completes specific tasks based on your instructions |
| 🤝 **Augmentation** | You and AI collaborate as creative thinking and task execution partners |
| 🧭 **Agency** | You configure AI to work independently on your behalf |

## 💡 Key Concepts

**Why AI Fluency matters:**
- AI tools change constantly — fluency skills stay relevant regardless of which model or platform you use
- Most people underuse AI not because of bad tools, but because of unclear thinking about how to collaborate
- The 4D framework gives a repeatable mental model for any AI interaction

**What fluency looks like in practice:**
- 🎯 *Delegation* → Knowing when AI adds value vs. when human judgment is essential
- 📝 *Description* → Writing prompts that give Claude the context it needs to succeed
- 🔍 *Discernment* → Reading outputs critically, verifying facts, catching errors
- 🛡️ *Diligence* → Protecting privacy, respecting IP, being honest about AI use
