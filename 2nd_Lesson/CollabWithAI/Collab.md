## 🧠 Lesson 1 — Your First Conversation with Claude

### 🎯 The 3 Elements of a Great Prompt

| Element | Question to Ask Yourself | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Setting the Stage** | What's my role & context? | *"I'm a marketing lead at a startup..."* |
| **Defining the Task** | What action do I want? | *"...research the streaming market..."* |
| **Specifying Rules** | What style/format do I need? | *"...structured as a professional report."* |

### 💡 The "Coworker Approach"
Talk to Claude **naturally, concisely, and conversationally** — like messaging a smart coworker, not typing a Google search.

### 🔄 Iterating on Responses
- Ask for more detail → *"Can you expand on point 2?"*
- Give feedback → *"Good, but make it more casual."*
- Redirect → *"Actually, I meant X, not Y."*

### 📎 Adding Context with Uploads
Claude can read: **PDF, DOCX, TXT, CSV, PNG, JPEG, and code files.**

## 🎯 Lesson 2 — Getting Better Results

### 🐛 Common Challenges & How to Fix Them

| Problem | Why It Happens | The Fix |
|---|---|---|
| **Too generic** | Not enough context in prompt | Add your role, audience & constraints |
| **Too long / too short** | Claude is guessing at length | Be explicit: *"Keep this under 100 words"* |
| **Wrong format** | Claude knows WHAT but not HOW | Show an example or describe structure clearly |
| **Wrong information** | Claude can occasionally be wrong | Verify facts independently for high-stakes work |
| **Wrong tone** | Claude defaults to professional | Say exactly what you want: *"More conversational"* |

### 🔁 The Iteration Mindset
> *"Your first prompt is the start of a conversation, not a one-shot request."*

✅ Treat first drafts as **starting points**, not final answers  
✅ Give **specific feedback** → *"Cut the first two paragraphs"* not just *"Make it shorter"*  
✅ Know when to **start fresh** → sometimes a new chat with a clearer prompt is faster

## 🏆 The 4D Framework for AI Fluency

Developed by **Professor Rick Dakan** (Ringling College) & **Professor Joseph Feller** (University College Cork):

| Competency | What It Means | In Practice |
|---|---|---|
| 🎯 **Delegation** | Decide what AI does vs. what humans do | Choose tasks strategically |
| 📝 **Description** | Communicate clearly with AI | Write great prompts |
| 🔍 **Discernment** | Critically evaluate AI outputs | Spot errors & improve quality |
| 🛡️ **Diligence** | Use AI responsibly & ethically | Stay accountable for AI-assisted work |

## 🧪 How to Run Evals (Test Claude on YOUR Work)

**Evals** = testing how well Claude handles tasks specific to *your* workflow.

### Simple 4-Step Eval Process:
1. **Gather examples** → Collect 5–10 real tasks you do regularly
2. **Create test prompts** → Write prompts that would produce similar outputs
3. **Compare outputs** → Ask: Is the info right? Is the tone correct? What's missing?
4. **Refine your approach** → Adjust prompts, add examples, flag where human review is needed

## 🔬 Practice Prompt (Built with the 3-Element Framework)

```
"I'm the marketing lead at an indie streaming startup, preparing an investor 
pitch deck. Research the current state of the independent film streaming market 
and identify key trends, competitor positioning, and growth opportunities. 
Use current web research with citations, structured as a professional report."
```

✅ **Stage set** → indie streaming startup, investor pitch  
✅ **Task defined** → research trends, competitors, opportunities  
✅ **Rules specified** → web citations, professional report format
